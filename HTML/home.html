Biryani

Biryani is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, goat, prawn and fish etc.), or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.

Ingredients for biryani : bay leaves, coriander,ginger, onions, tomatoes, green chilies and garlic. The main Ingredient is chicken.

Potato Rosti

In grated potatoes add the egg, parsley, spring onion, sweet paprika, coriander, salt, pepper, and cornstarch.Mix it and deepfry it.

Ingredients for rostis : potatoes, eggs, parsley, spring onion, sweet paprika, coriander, salt, pepper, cornstarch, oil for frying

Shawarma

Shawarma is vastly popular across the middle-east, specially Lebanon. Now, a popular street food as well, chicken shawarma has quickly garnered appreciation across the globe, particularly in India. You can prepare this easy shawarma at home as a side dish for brunch or for evening snacks.
Ingredients in Chicken Shawarma: Chicken baked in a yogurt marinade, rolled up in pita bread and served with tahini or mayonnaise. This seems like the perfect dish for your evening binge eating.
